Admissions Process

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and inclusive. We welcome applications from students of diverse backgrounds and abilities. Prospective students and their families are encouraged to schedule a visit to our campus, meet with our admissions team, and learn more about our programs. The application process includes submitting an online application form, providing relevant academic records, and scheduling an interview or assessment for the student. We work closely with families to ensure a smooth transition into our school community At JAM DAM School, we believe in an inclusive admissions process that provides equal opportunity to all prospective students..

Scholarships and Financial Aid

We believe that financial constraints should not hinder any child’s educational journey. To support deserving students, we offer a range of scholarships and financial aid programs based on merit, need, or special talents in areas such as arts and athletics. Our scholarship programs are designed to ensure that every student has access to quality education, irrespective of their financial background.
