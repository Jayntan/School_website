School Infrastructure

Our campus is designed to provide a conducive learning environment. Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms, fully equipped science labs, a well-stocked library, and computer labs with the latest technology create an environment where students can immerse themselves in learning. The campus is also equipped with high-speed internet to facilitate research and technology-based learning.

Bus Transport

JAM DAM School offers a safe and reliable school bus transport system that covers a wide area, ensuring that students can commute comfortably from their homes to school. All buses are equipped with GPS tracking, and we ensure strict safety protocols, with trained drivers and attendants on board. We also offer real-time updates for parents so they can track their child’s journey and ensure timely arrivals and departures.

Sports Facilities

Our school provides state-of-the-art sports facilities that cater to both team and individual sports. With fields for cricket, football, and athletics, as well as indoor facilities for badminton, basketball, and table tennis, we ensure that students have ample opportunities to stay active and develop physical fitness. We also have a dedicated swimming pool and a trained coaching staff to support students in their athletic development.

Creative Arts Studio

The Creative Arts Studio at JAM DAM School is a hub of innovation and creativity. Our well-equipped art rooms provide students with the resources they need to experiment with different mediums, from painting and sculpting to photography and digital art. Our music room is home to a variety of instruments, enabling students to explore their musical talents and participate in school performances.
